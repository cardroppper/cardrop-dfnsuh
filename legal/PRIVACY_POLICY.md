
# Privacy Policy for CarDrop

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

CarDrop ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, username, display name, profile photo
- **Vehicle Information**: Make, model, year, modifications, photos of your vehicles
- **Location Data**: Real-time location for nearby vehicle detection and meet discovery
- **Bluetooth Data**: Beacon identifiers for proximity detection

### Usage Information
- App interactions and feature usage
- Device information (model, OS version, unique identifiers)
- Log data and crash reports

### User-Generated Content
- Photos and videos uploaded to vehicle profiles, timelines, and meet galleries
- Messages sent through private messaging and club chats
- Event RSVPs and attendance data

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain CarDrop services
- Enable proximity-based vehicle detection
- Facilitate club memberships and event coordination
- Send notifications about nearby vehicles and club activities
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Public Information
- Vehicle profiles (if set to public)
- Club memberships (for public clubs)
- Event attendance (for events you RSVP to)

### Private Information
We do NOT share your private information except:
- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect rights, property, or safety
- In connection with business transfers (mergers, acquisitions)

### Third-Party Services
We use:
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication
- **Expo**: App infrastructure and push notifications
- **Analytics providers**: Anonymous usage statistics

## Bluetooth and Location

- **Bluetooth**: Used only for detecting nearby CarDrop beacons. We do not access other Bluetooth devices.
- **Location**: Used to show nearby vehicles and meets. You can disable location access in settings, but proximity features will be limited.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication with hashed passwords
- Row-level security on database
- Regular security audits

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Restrict data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@cardrop.app

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer for compliance

## Children's Privacy

CarDrop is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will delete it immediately.

## International Users

CarDrop is operated in the United States. By using the app, you consent to data transfer to the US, which may have different privacy laws than your jurisdiction.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via:
- In-app notification
- Email to registered address
- Updated "Last Updated" date

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed

- Right to deletion
- Right to non-discrimination

EU/EEA residents have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

- Legal basis for processing: Consent, contract performance, legitimate interests
